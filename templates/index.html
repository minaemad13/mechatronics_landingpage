About Us

The company offers innovative solutions to ensure its customers' operations run efficiently according to the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

The scope of Mechatronics activities covers industrial electronics boards, repair of kitchen and laundry equipment, provision of original spare parts, maintenance contracts and after-sales services under the Equipment Care Program (ECP) which results in longer lasting equipment, greater operational efficiency and safety.

With a professional team; Mechatronics is able to provide many tailored solutions for commercial kitchens, laundries and ship kitchens, in diverse applications ranging from hotels, luxury hotels, restaurants, ships, cruise ships, airlines, labor camps, military, healthcare institutions, laundromats, and commercial laundromats.
